OBL Financial Working Capital Service
Working Capital Service

Instead of having to wait 45-60 days to receive payment, a Merchant Cash Advance involves using your past credit card sales to predict your future credit card sales and borrowing those future sales dollars for cash today.

While a Working Capital Loan is pledging owned assets as collateral and that collateral for cash today.

If you have at least 12 months in business, OBL can provide these services to assist with unexpected cash outlays or assisting with temporary financial problems.
